How it was scored

Each answer’s quality = 55% fact-recall + 20% relevance + 15% specificity + 10% non-deflection. Fact recall checks whether the site’s key facts (prices, data caps, speeds, programme counts) appear in the answer, matched on normalised text. A deflection is a clarify-first reply or human-agent hand-off that never states the fact.

Source of truth: telekom.si product pages (/mobilno, /internet, /neo) plus the published price lists (/pomoc/ceniki), captured July 2026. All 300 questions were asked live via the public Boost.ai Chat API, one fresh conversation each. Fully reproducible from the saved transcript with evaluate.py --rescore results.json.
